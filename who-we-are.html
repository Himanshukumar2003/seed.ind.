Who We Are

Established in 2006 with Head Office in New Delhi & Projects Implemented Pan India, Specialising in Implementing Social Projects

Established as a Registered Society in the year 2006, SEED has grown into a large and trusted brand that specialises in implementing CSR & Social Intervention projects for companies, both in the public and private sector.

A committed team supports the top leadership to achieve the overall vision of ensuring equitable growth and opportunities for those living in hard-to-reach areas as marginalised and disadvantaged population groups.

Providing the entire basket of services as end-to-end solutions, SEED undertakes a complete assessment of the clientâ€™s nature of business and proposes interventions that are backed by Need Assessment, Formative Research, Baseline Studies and Assessment Practices (KAP) studies.

Systematic approaches are followed, to develop credible models with potential for impact and change. Broadly, thematic areas covered are Rural Development, Community Mobilisation, Quality Education, Livelihood Development, Health, Agriculture Development, WASH, Employable Skill Development, and Women Empowerment.

A lot of work has also been done on COVID-19, supporting our patrons to reach out to sections of society in desperate need.

We Facilitate Social Interventions

Social Project Interventions

The Research & Survey team conducts Social Impact Assessment which includes the processes of analyzing, monitoring and managing the intended and unintended social consequences, both positive and negative, of planned interventions and any social change processes invoked by those interventions.

The main outcome of Social Impact Assessment surveys includes getting insights about the impact of the facilities provided to the community. Based on the analyzation of the data captured, necessary actions are suggested to rectify the problems faced and implement necessary Project activities.

Baseline Needs & Assessment Study

To perform CSR & Social Project activities effectively, the survey team at SEED CSR conducts Baseline Surveys and Needs Assessments to understand key issues in the targeted project area. They provide valuable insights into the issues faced by the population groups residing in those geographies.

The main outcome of Need Assessment surveys includes getting insights about the needs and problems of the community both at individual household as well as village level related to thematic areas of WASH, Education, Health etc. Based on the analyzation of these needs and problems, further implementation of the various necessary Social Intervention activities in the respective surveyed locations takes place.

Social Audit And Sustainability Report

Social Auditing is conducted on behalf of partner corporates through continuous auditing and inspection. All the results and findings are systematically mapped and documented in the Social Audit and Sustainability Report.

Social audit is conducted as a part of Social Impact Assessment Survey, in which the Research & Survey team reviews the official records and analyzes whether the reported expenditures by the implementing partners matches the actual money spent on the ground.

Advisory & Advocacy

Based on our years of experience in the Social sector we help organisations develop their policies and road map for Social Interventions. The intent is to ensure the vision of the Organisation is aligned to the projects taken up addressing the Target Groups. It is just not enough to spend your funds and resources but to spend them judiciously.

With experience also comes responsibility. SEED thus believes that we must share our expertise with the Authorities that matter to make policy decisions that govern this nation. We try and work as closely as possible with Administrations at various levels to help make the right and definitive steps forward that positively impacts the Society at large.
