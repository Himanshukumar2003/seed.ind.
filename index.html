Who We Are

SEED is India’s leading integrated CSR & Social Intervention implementing agency offering a range of services to businesses across sectors that include energy, banking, financial services & insurance (BFSI), oil & gas, steel & mines, cement, infrastructure, manufacturing, IT & ITES, pharmaceuticals, automobiles, aviation, healthcare, hospitality and logistics, amongst others.

Set up as a Registered Society in the year 2006, SEED has today grown into a large and trusted brand that specialises in implementing CSR & Social Intervention projects for companies, both in the public and private sector. A committed team supports the top leadership to achieve the overall vision of ensuring equitable growth and opportunities for those living in hard-to-reach areas as marginalised and disadvantaged population groups.

Providing the entire basket of services as end-to-end solutions, SEED undertakes a complete assessment of the client’s nature of business and proposes interventions that are backed by needs assessment, formative research, baseline studies and other knowledge, assessment, practices (KAP) studies. Systematic approaches are followed, to develop credible models with potential for impact and change. Broadly, thematic areas covered are rural development, community mobilisation, quality education, livelihood development, health, agriculture development, Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH), employable skill development and women empowerment.

FAQ’s for SEED

We appreciate the curiosity and interest people show in SEED’s work. To help answer some of the most common inquiries, we’ve created this FAQ section. Whether you’re interested in volunteering, supporting our initiatives, or learning more about our impact areas like education, health, and livelihood, these answers should help clarify our mission and programs.

Learn how to get help

What is SEED’s mission?

SEED aims to empower marginalized communities by addressing education, health, and livelihood needs through sustainable projects.

What programs does SEED offer?

SEED implements programs in areas like education, health, water and sanitation, livelihood development, and rural infrastructure.

Who does SEED partner with ?

SEED collaborates with corporate partners to design and execute Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects that create lasting impact.

How can I support SEED’s work?

You can support SEED by donating, volunteering, or partnering with them on various projects.
