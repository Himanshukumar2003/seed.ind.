We Care

What We Care For!

"We cannot help everyone, but everyone can help someone"

It is hard to think of a single section of society that is not impacted by the Covid-19 pandemic, the effects of which the world will continue to feel for a long time to come. In these challenging times, it is difficult to think of anyone apart from one’s own families and their survival. The aftermath of the pandemic entails a speculative future for economies and industries but, in particular, the poor and marginalized communities. The most affected are the daily wage earners who are left abandoned with access to limited or no resources.

With the increase in day-to-day challenges for survival, and adverse financial consequences to the economy of almost every nation, this outbreak has resulted in a trying situation for the marginalized sections of society. Amidst these severely restrictive circumstances, we strongly believe in giving back to the less fortunate. Strictly following every preventive measure for Covid-19, our team has kept these communities actively engaged and is helping them fight against this pandemic.

As masks have become an essential requirement in these times, the SEED team has been involved in training women help groups to make Covid-19 facemasks through online classes and self-made craft videos. We have also delivered bundles of cloth to these groups to physically make these facemasks. We are giving them the support to develop and augment their skills while keeping them protected from this pandemic.

The SEED team has conducted a ‘Mask Distribution Drive’ in Janauli village, Palwal with the help of Mr. Surjender. Hind Terminals helped in distributing ration to 100 tribal families in Savroli village, Raigarh. All these activities have been implemented following proper social distancing and sanitization procedures.

We are trying to reach as many areas as we can to support the deprived sections of our society. In this context, with the help of Mr. Jahangir, our vendor and HCL project staff, the SEED HO team has distributed food packets near Charbagh railway station and Topdarwaza community, Lucknow.

We strongly believe in helping others and remain committed in our efforts to mitigate the difficulties faced by the poor and the vulnerable. These small steps cannot relieve them from the pandemic but they will give them a fighting chance against its detrimental effects. Please continue to support us so that we can continue to serve the people.

Interventions

We Facilitate Social Interventions

Social Project Interventions

The Research & Survey team conducts Social Impact Assessment which includes the processes of analyzing, monitoring and managing the intended and unintended social consequences, both positive and negative, of planned interventions and any social change processes invoked by those interventions.

The main outcome of Social Impact Assessment surveys includes getting insights about the impact of the facilities provided to the community. Based on the analyzation of the data captured, necessary actions are suggested to rectify the problems faced and implement necessary Project activities.

Baseline Needs & Assessment Study

To perform CSR & Social Project activities effectively, the survey team at SEED CSR conducts Baseline Surveys and Needs Assessments to understand key issues in the targeted project area. They provide valuable insights into the issues faced by the population groups residing in those geographies.

The main outcome of Need Assessment surveys includes getting insights about the needs and problems of the community both at individual household as well as village level related to thematic areas of WASH, Education, Health etc. Based on the analyzation of these needs and problems, further implementation of the various necessary Social Intervention activities in the respective surveyed locations takes place.

Social Audit And Sustainability Report

Social Auditing is conducted on behalf of partner corporates through continuous auditing and inspection. All the results and findings are systematically mapped and documented in the Social Audit and Sustainability Report.

Social audit is conducted as a part of Social Impact Assessment Survey, in which the Research & Survey team reviews the official records and analyzes whether the reported expenditures by the implementing partners matches the actual money spent on the ground.

Advisory & Advocacy

Based on our years of experience in the Social sector we help organisations develop their policies and road map for Social Interventions. The intent is to ensure the vision of the Organisation is aligned to the projects taken up addressing the Target Groups. It is just not enough to spend your funds and resources but to spend them judiciously.

With experience also comes responsibility. SEED thus believes that we must share our expertise with the Authorities that matter to make policy decisions that govern this nation. We try and work as closely as possible with Administrations at various levels to help make the right and definitive steps forward that positively impacts the Society at large.

SEED is dedicated to making a meaningful impact through transformative initiatives in education, healthcare, and economic development. With a focus on empowering communities, we strive to create lasting change and a brighter future for all
